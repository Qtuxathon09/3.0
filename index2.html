About QTUXATHON 3.0

QTUXATHON 3.0 is a national-level hackathon designed to challenge and inspire the brightest minds in technology. Building on the success of our previous editions, this year's hackathon focuses on pushing the boundaries of innovation in cybersecurity, AI, blockchain, and sustainable tech.

Participants will have the opportunity to collaborate, learn from experts, and develop groundbreaking solutions to real-world problems. Whether you're a seasoned coder or a budding enthusiast, QTUXATHON 3.0 provides a platform to showcase your skills, network with peers, and make a tangible impact.

Join us for an exhilarating experience filled with intense coding, creative problem-solving, and unforgettable moments. Let's break the limits together!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in QTUXATHON 3.0?

QTUXATHON 3.0 is open to all college students (undergraduate and postgraduate) from any discipline, as well as recent graduates and tech enthusiasts. If you have a passion for innovation and problem-solving, you're welcome!

Is the hackathon online or offline?

QTUXATHON 3.0 will be a hybrid event. The initial rounds and idea submission will be online, while the final hack day and judging will be conducted offline at a designated venue. More details on the venue will be shared soon.

What is the maximum team size?

Teams can consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members. We encourage diverse teams with varied skill sets to maximize creativity and collaboration.

Do I get a certificate for participation?

Yes, all participants who successfully submit a valid project will receive a digital certificate of participation. Winners and top teams will receive additional certificates and prizes.

What are the themes for the hackathon?

The primary themes for QTUXATHON 3.0 include Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Blockchain & Web3, and Sustainable Technologies. Specific problem statements within these themes will be announced shortly after registrations open.
